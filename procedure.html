Description

Prepare LB Broth Agar Plates

Create different concentrations of the monoterpene fragrances

3

Culture S. epidermidis using a shaking water bath

4

Spread S. epidermidis culture on agar plates using an L-spreader

5

Apply filter discs soaked in monoterpene solutions to the agar plates

6

Place plates in the incubator for 24 hours

7

Measure the zones of inhibition using a dial caliper
